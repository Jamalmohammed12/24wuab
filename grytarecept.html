Chilli Gryta

  • 4 portioner
  • 30 min
  • Kött

    • Ingrediants

    • Chicken breasts (2)
    • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)
    • Garlic cloves (3, minced)
    • Onion (1, diced)
    • Bell pepper (1, sliced)
    • Tomatoes (2, chopped)
    • Salt (1 teaspoon)
    • Black pepper (½ teaspoon)

    Gör så här

    1. Prepare the Chicken: Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Sauté chicken until golden brown and cooked through, then set aside. Sauté the Vegetables:
    2. In the same skillet, add a bit more olive oil if needed. Sauté garlic, onion, and bell pepper until softened and fragrant. Add Tomatoes and Seasoning:
    3. Stir in chopped tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes until tomatoes soften slightly. Combine Chicken and Vegetables:
    4. Return chicken to the skillet. Spoon vegetable mixture over chicken. Simmer for a few more minutes to let flavors meld. Serve:
    5. Transfer chicken and vegetables to a serving dish. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your flavorfli dish!
